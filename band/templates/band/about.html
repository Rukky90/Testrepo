About Height Bros

Introduction

Height Bros is a pop band formed in 2010 in Cape Town, South Africa. Known for their distinctive sound and style, Height Bros has quickly risen to prominence in the pop music scene.

Members

  • Benny - Vocals, Guitar
  • Josh - Drums
  • Moore - Keyboard

Music Style

Our music blends influences from various styles, creating a unique sound that resonates with fans of multiple genres. With powerful lyrics and captivating melodies, we aim to deliver an unforgettable experience through our songs.

Achievements

Since our formation, we have:

  • Released one album/EP, including Grace.
  • Performed at the O2 Arena in London.
  • Received an iHeart Award.
